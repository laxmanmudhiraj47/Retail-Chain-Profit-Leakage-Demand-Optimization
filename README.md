## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales, inventory, and profitability to identify **profit leakage** and improve **demand optimization**. Using **Power BI**, **Tableau**, **SQL**, **Python**, and **Excel**, the project transforms raw retail data into interactive dashboards and actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze sales and profit performance
- Identify profit leakage across products and regions
- Optimize inventory management
- Monitor product demand and stock levels
- Support business decisions with interactive dashboards
